### Full Description
BBHCBazaar is the official, secure campus-based marketplace app designed exclusively for B.B. Hegde College's students, faculty, and staff. The application is built to foster entrepreneurship and highlight local campus talent.

At BBHCBazaar, student life meets innovation. Our platform allows students to easily act as both sellers and buyers within a trusted, verified ecosystem:

* Show Your Talent: Student creators, craftsmakers, software developers, chess coaches, and growers can list their physical products (like handmade crafts, fresh garden vegetables, software tools, or art) or services directly in the app.
* Seamless Browsing & Ordering: Buyers can browse available products and services, request custom orders, and make secure institutional purchases directly from fellow student peers.
* The Campus Outlet Verification System: We ensure safe transactions through our dedicated on-campus physical outlet. No random meetups are needed:
  1. Once a student seller accepts an order, both the buyer and seller receive unique, secure QR codes.
  2. The seller drops the product off at the physical campus shop (the Outlet).
  3. The outlet manager scans the seller's QR code to verify and accept the item. An automatic notification is instantly sent to the buyer.
  4. The buyer visits the outlet, scans their QR code, and the outlet manager hands over the verified product.
* Build Entrepreneurial Skills: Gain real-world business experience in pricing, inventory, marketing, and logistics.

BBHCBazaar is designed to create a vibrant micro-economy in our college, helping students turn their hobbies and skills into a functioning business while keeping all transactions safe, reliable, and verified.
